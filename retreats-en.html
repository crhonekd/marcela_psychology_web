The Reset Retreat:
Coming Home to Yourself

A therapeutic retreat for women entering a new season of life.

October 2026  ·  Spain

An invitation to slow down

This retreat is an invitation to slow down. To step away from daily responsibilities and expectations. To reconnect with yourself. To spend time with women who understand that life is changing — and who are ready to embrace this next chapter with greater ease, confidence, and self-respect.

Together, we will explore the physical, emotional, and psychological changes that often arise during this stage of life. Through expert guidance, meaningful conversations, gentle movement, nourishing food, and restorative rest, you will gain a deeper understanding of what your mind and body may be asking of you now. Hormonal changes can be hard — but you don't have to face it alone.

Who is this retreat for?

  • — Women who feel that life is asking something different from them
  • — Women who are tired of constantly pushing through
  • — Women who want to understand their changing body with greater compassion
  • — Women who are ready to stop putting themselves last
  • — Women who long for space, peace, genuine connection, and renewal

No previous experience with yoga, retreats, or personal development is needed.

What to expect

  • Insightful workshops led by a psychologist
  • Practical understanding of hormonal and emotional changes
  • Gentle yoga, movement, stretching and Pilates for all levels
  • Relaxation, reflection and guided sharing for inner calm
  • Beautiful surroundings designed to help you slow down
  • Nourishing meals prepared with wellbeing in mind
  • Genuine connection with like-minded women
  • Plenty of time to rest, explore, laugh, and simply be looked after

Most importantly, you will discover that you do not have to carry everything alone.

The experience

Intentional Structure

We blend gentle exercise, open exploration, and space to reflect — so the experience feels both focused and fluid. No rushing. No pressure. No expectations.

Collaborative Energy

Connection is a core part of the process. You'll learn just as much from the group as from the content itself. Reconnect with your body and mind as you escape the noise of everyday life.

Expert Facilitation

Led by an experienced psychologist who knows how to hold space, encourage participation, and keep things moving with purpose and warmth.

A Supportive Space

This retreat prioritises comfort, safety, and respect — so you can show up exactly as you are and fully engage in the process of healing and renewal.

A place to breathe

"Not every woman needs therapy — but every woman deserves a place where she can be herself."

A place where she can slow down without guilt. Be supported without having to earn it. Be cared for without having to ask. And remember that this chapter of life is not an ending — it may be the beginning of something beautiful.

Frequently asked questions

Can I come alone?

Absolutely. Many people come on their own, and often, those are the ones who leave with the strongest connections. This retreat is designed to create real community. You'll feel welcomed, included, and gently supported from the very beginning.

What if I'm not flexible or experienced in yoga?

No problem at all. All movement practices are gentle, accessible, and adapted to all levels — including complete beginners. It's not about performance, it's about connecting with yourself. And having some fun too!

Who will I be sharing a room with?

You'll be paired with someone kind, open-minded, and likely on a similar journey. Most guests come solo — and many leave with a new friend for life. If you have any concerns, just reach out and we can talk it through.

Will there be free time to explore or rest?

Yes — absolutely. While there's a rich and beautiful programme, everything is optional. You're free to rest, wander, journal, or simply breathe whenever you need to. This is your time, and you get to choose what feels right for you in each moment.

What happens after the retreat ends?

The clarity, connection, and insight you gain tend to ripple into your everyday life long after you return home. You'll be part of a private group where you can stay in touch with fellow participants, and optional follow-up support will be available to help you keep the momentum going.
